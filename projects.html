  2. Speed limit sign classification

    Autonomous vehicles are future of the world and emerging day by day. IN this project we are going to attain to perform the level 2 of Autonomous vehicles using the sign board classification. Based on classification of sign board speed limit the speed of vehicle is changed autonomously. Deep learning CNN algorithm along with OpenCV is used in this project

    Important tools and frameworks

    Python, Keras, Tensorow, OpenCV.





  3. Door automation and Energy management

    Automated door opening for the registered user using Face recognition and turning ON lights and fans and turning OFF lights and fans when the room is empty. Unknown users are not allowed to enter in the room. Raspberry pi is used as microprocessor.

    Important tools and frameworks

    Raspbian OS, Python, OpenCV, Face recognition,Tensorflow
